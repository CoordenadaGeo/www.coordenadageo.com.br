Who we are

At the interface of science, technology and management

Coordenada Geo is a consultancy specialised in geospatial technologies that turns spatial data into applied knowledge to support strategic decisions about the territory. We develop analysis, diagnostics and tailor-made solutions, guided by scientific rigour, the Agile Manifesto and close client collaboration. Our foundations are trust, technical precision and transparency.

  • Intelligent, objective and accessible communication — didactic and collaborative.
  • Scientific rigour combined with iterative delivery inspired by the Agile Manifesto.
  • A team that conveys trust, precision and transparency on every project.

Purpose

Applied knowledge for more conscious decisions about the territory

To turn geographic data into applied knowledge that supports more conscious decisions about the territory. We integrate science, technology and technical experience to make spatial information clear, accessible and useful.

Values

What guides us

Technical precision

Scientific rigour when choosing methods, datasets and validations, without sacrificing clarity.

Collaboration

We build solutions close to the client, integrating technical and domain expertise.

Adaptability

We adjust scope, tools and deliverables to the reality of each project and scale.

Social-environmental purpose

We prioritise initiatives that generate positive impact on people, territories and ecosystems.

Services

End-to-end applied geospatial technology

Tailor-made solutions in remote sensing, geoprocessing and spatial analysis — from assessment to operation.

Remote sensing

Image classification, land use and land cover monitoring, change detection and time series.

Geoprocessing and spatial analysis

Geographic data modelling, map algebra, spatial statistics and territorial studies.

Dashboards and visualisation

Interactive maps and web panels to follow indicators, areas of interest and time series.

Automation and data pipelines

Geospatial ETL, integration with public APIs and orchestration of collection, processing and publishing.

Custom geospatial platforms

Development of geospatial web applications and APIs, from prototype to production.

Training and technical support

Training, code reviews and mentoring for teams working with geographic data.

SaaS platform

Our geospatial platform to operate at scale

We build and operate a proprietary SaaS platform that brings geospatial technologies into our clients day-to-day: layer catalogue, automations, APIs, dashboards and dedicated workspaces — with on-demand access and plans aligned to the agreed scope.
