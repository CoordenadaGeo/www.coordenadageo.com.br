## A new point of reference

This is the first post from Coordenada Geo. We will use this space to share studies, lessons
learned and the behind-the-scenes of our geotechnology projects — always focused on turning
geographic data into applied knowledge and more conscious decisions about the territory.

## What to expect

- **Technical studies** with real territorial analyses.
- **Integrations** with Brazilian public datasets (CAR, IBAMA, MapBiomas, Varda).
- **Platform engineering**: PostGIS, H3, ingestion pipelines.
- **Reflections** on science, technology and socio-environmental impact.
